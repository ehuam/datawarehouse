* Part 1: ETF Master Collection — Building the Universe of ETFs
Objective:
Create a comprehensive collection of ALL ETFs with their basic metadata.
Store all ETFs now, filter for Equity ETFs later when building holdings.
** Primary Data fields:
1. **AUM (assets under management)** — you'll use this to filter for large Equity ETFs.
2. price
3. volume
4. asset class
5. expense ratio
** Other fields to consider:
1. symbol
2. fund name
3. asset class
4. sector
5. industry
6. country
7. market cap
8. Price to Earnings ratio
9. change
10. volume
